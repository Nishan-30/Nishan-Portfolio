Selected Projects

VELOXIS

AI-powered traffic analysis platform built for mixed-traffic conditions in Bangladesh. Combines a custom-trained YOLOv11 model with advanced traffic engineering metrics including vehicle counting, PHF, saturation flow, LOS, V85, queue length, and turning movement counts. Designed for researchers and engineers seeking accurate, low-cost, locally-adapted traffic data.

Model Training Update

Successfully trained the YOLOv11 model on 5,000+ locally sourced images of mixed traffic in Dhaka. Achieved high accuracy in detecting rickshaws, CNGs, and buses.

Completed extensive video data collection at 10 major intersections. This data is being used to calibrate the saturation flow models for local conditions.
