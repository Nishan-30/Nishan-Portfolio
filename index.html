Selected Projects

VELOXIS

VELOXIS is an AI-powered traffic analysis platform specifically built for mixed-traffic conditions in Bangladesh. It combines a custom-trained YOLOv11 model with robust tracking and advanced traffic engineering metrics — including vehicle counting, PHF, saturation flow, LOS, V85, queue length, and turning movement counts. Designed for researchers and engineers, VELOXIS delivers accurate, low-cost, and locally-adapted traffic data using nothing more than a laptop and smartphone camera.
